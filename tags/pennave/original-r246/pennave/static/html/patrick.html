Welcome to my collection of photos on the web. As time goes on, I'll add more photos to this list. For right now, you just get this simple and messy welcome page. You can choose one of the sets below, or feel free to browse all my photos.

My Favorite Photos

For one reason or another, I've decided that these photos are my favorites. Don't ask me why, I guess they just hold a special place in my heart or something like that.

Boston GNOME Summit 2006

October 7-9, 2006 - I got to attend the 2006 Boston GNOME Summit as a strange combination between a participant and a researcher. Along the way, I got a chance to see some parts of Boston too.

Mary and Wendell's Wedding

July 21-23, 2006 - Photos from our wonderful weekend in Big Rapids, MI at Mary Hannink and Wendell Holmes' wedding.

Niagara Falls Anniversary Trip

July 9-10, 2006 - Kristina and I took a quick trip up to Niagara Falls for our second anniversary.

Bellefield Honduras Mission 2006

June 16-24, 2006 - As part of our church's continuing mission to San Jose, Honduras, Kristina and I travelled there with a team for a little over a week in June of 2006. While there we worked with the children, did some construction, built relationships, and helped solidify the church's position with the village.

May 2006 Minnesota Trip

May 27-June 2,2006 - We took a trip of Michigan for the wedding of a few friends and realized that we could just tack on a few more miles and drive to Minnesota. While we were there we took a side trip up to Duluth to wander around the harbor and Jay Cooke State Park.

Genoa, Italy and OSS 2005

July 10-July 16, 2005 - I presented at OSS 2005 in Genoa, Italy. While there, I had a nice chance to wander around the ancient city and local area.

Our Wedding

July 10, 2004 - Kristina and I were married on July 10th, 2004 at Palm Valley Lutheran Church in Round Rock, Texas. Afterwards we had a small reception and then took a two week honeymoon to Galveston and the Caribbean. Right now these photos are just the photos from us and the official photographer. At some point in time, I'll find my collection of photos from everyone else and put those online too.
